What does the timeline look like​?

  1. You contact us for a consult and we schedule a time to meet.
  2. We have the initial meeting where we discuss what your needs are. We'll ask questions like: "Would you like help managing your social media pages?" and "Would you like clients to be able to book appointments with you directly on your site?" This way we'll be able to gauge which package is best for you! In this initial meeting we'll agree upon a price and all of the features you'd like your site to include.
  3. In the same day as our initial meeting, we'll send you a detailed proposal of everything we've discussed; think of this as the blue-print for your site.
  4. After you approve the proposal, we will get to work right away! We'll send you different fonts & color pallettes to choose from & have the first draft of your site completed within 2 weeks.
  5. After the first draft of the site has been reveiwed by you, we'll take your feedback and incoprporate it into the second and third drafts of the site - until it looks just right. Depending on what revisions are requested by you, this step will take 2-3 weeks!
  6. After the site looks perfect to you, we will have a pre-launch meeting with you where we will go over all of the site's unique functions together!
  7. Right after this meeting, the site will go live!
  8. Depending on the package that's right for you, expect us to make any requested updates on your site for 1-3 hours a month! We'll always be there for any tech-support you need.

Have your new site in 4-6 weeks from the first time you call us!
